# Corollary 1

If a system (G,w) is a resource allocation problem in 𝒢_n × {w}, then selecting p_ζ ∝ ν_ζ⋆ / ∑_{ψ∈[k]} (n choose ψ) ν_ψ⋆ for all ζ∈[k] gives 𝔼[1/T ∑_{t=1}^T W(a^t)] ≥ (T-1)/(2T) * SPoA_k(𝒢_n,w) * W(a^opt).
