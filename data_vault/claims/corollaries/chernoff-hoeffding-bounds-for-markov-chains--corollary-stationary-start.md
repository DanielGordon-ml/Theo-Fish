# Corollary (stationary start)

When the initial distribution φ equals the stationary distribution π, the term ‖φ/π‖_π = 1, and the bounds in Theorem 1.1 become Pr[X ≥ μ + δ] ≤ exp(−(1−λ(M))δ²t/2) and Pr[X ≤ μ − δ] ≤ exp(−(1−λ(M))δ²t/2).
