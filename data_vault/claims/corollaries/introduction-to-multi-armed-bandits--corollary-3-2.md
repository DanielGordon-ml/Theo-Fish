# Corollary 3.2

$\mathbb{P}_{H}=\mathbb{P}_{H^{\prime}}$ whenever $H^{\prime}=\left(\left(a^{\prime}_{\sigma(t)},\,r^{\prime}_{\sigma(t)}\right):\;t\in[T]\right)$ for some permutation $\sigma$ of $[t]$.
