# Corollary 9.6

$\tfrac{1}{T}\,\operatorname{\mathbb{E}}[\mathtt{cost}(\mathtt{ALG})]=\tfrac{1}{T}\,\operatorname{\mathbb{E}}[\mathtt{rew}(\mathtt{ADV})]\in\left[v^{*}-\tfrac{\operatorname{\mathbb{E}}[R'(T)]}{T},\,v^{*}+\tfrac{\operatorname{\mathbb{E}}[R(T)]}{T}\right].$
Moreover, the expected average play $(\operatorname{\mathbb{E}}[\bar{\imath}],\,\operatorname{\mathbb{E}}[\bar{\jmath}])$ forms an $\operatorname{\mathbb{E}}[\epsilon_{T}]$-approximate Nash equilibrium.
