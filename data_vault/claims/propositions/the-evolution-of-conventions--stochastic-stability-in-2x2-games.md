# Stochastic stability in 2x2 games

If R_1 > R_2, then the unique stochastically stable convention is h_1 for all sufficiently large values of k and m/k. If R_1 = R_2, then both h_1 and h_2 are stochastically stable conventions for all sufficiently large values of k and m/k.
