# Proposition V.3 (Comparison with RSW/IK)

Theorem I.2 improves upon Theorem V.4 in three ways: (1) fi can be real-valued in [0,1] instead of Boolean {0,1}; (2) the constant in the exponent is 1/3 instead of 1/4; (3) the prefactor is ‖φ/π‖_π instead of 2/ε. Moreover, the result extends to irreversible chains.
