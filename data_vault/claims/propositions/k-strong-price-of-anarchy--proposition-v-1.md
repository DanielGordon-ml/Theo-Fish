# Proposition V.1

A system (G, W, U) that is (λ, μ)-k-generalized-coalitionally smooth has k-strong price of anarchy satisfying SPoA_k(G, W, U) ≥ λ_ζ / (1 + μ_ζ), ∀ ζ ∈ [k].
