# Exercise 9.3(c)

Suppose $\mathtt{ADV}$ is the best-response adversary, as per Eq. (122), and $\mathtt{ALG}$ is $\mathtt{Hedge}$ with full feedback and parameter $\epsilon = \sqrt{(\ln K)/(2T)}$. Assume that $M$ is a $K \times K$ matrix with entries in $[0,1]$. Then $(\bar{\imath}, \bar{\jmath})$ forms a $\delta_T$-approximate Nash equilibrium where $\delta_T = O\left(\sqrt{\frac{\ln(KT)}{T}}\right)$.
