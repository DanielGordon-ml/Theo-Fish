# Sinks of best-reply graph are strict Nash equilibria

Every sink of the best reply graph is clearly a strict Nash equilibrium in pure strategies. So a game is weakly acyclic if, and only if, from every strategy-tuple there exists a finite sequence of best replies by one agent at a time that ends in a strict, pure strategy Nash equilibrium.
