# Proposition (Mixing time connection)

For an ergodic Markov chain M with spectral expansion λ(M), the ε-mixing time satisfies T(ε) = O(log(n/ε)/(1−λ(M))). Thus the concentration bound in Theorem 1.1 can be restated in terms of mixing time: the number of steps t needed for concentration is proportional to the mixing time.
