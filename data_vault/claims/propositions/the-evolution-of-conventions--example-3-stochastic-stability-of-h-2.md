# Example 3 — Stochastic stability of h_2

It is readily seen that the least resistant tree is rooted at h_2 and has total resistance [(1/8)k] + [(3/8)k]. For all sufficiently large k this is the unique tree of least resistance, so h_2 is the unique generically stable convention. The risk dominant equilibrium, however, is strategy 3. It is also the Pareto optimal equilibrium.
