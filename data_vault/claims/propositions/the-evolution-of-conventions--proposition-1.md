# Proposition 1

Let P^0 be the unperturbed adaptive play process for a weakly acyclic game Γ with memory m and sample size k ≤ m/(L+2). The recurrent communication classes of P^0 are exactly the conventions (constant histories corresponding to strict Nash equilibria of Γ).
