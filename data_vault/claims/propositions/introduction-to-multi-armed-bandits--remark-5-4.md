# Remark 5.4

The notion in (73), R(T) = cost(ALG) - min_{a ∈ [K]} cost(a), is usually referred to simply as regret in the literature, whereas (74), R(T) = cost(ALG) - min_{a ∈ [K]} E[cost(a)], is often called pseudo-regret.
