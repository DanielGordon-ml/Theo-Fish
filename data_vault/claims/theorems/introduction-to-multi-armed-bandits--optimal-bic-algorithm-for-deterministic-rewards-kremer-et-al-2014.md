# Optimal BIC algorithm for deterministic rewards (Kremer et al. 2014)

The special case of deterministic rewards and two arms has been optimally solved in the original paper of Kremer et al. (2014). That is, they design a BIC algorithm which exactly optimizes the expected reward, for a given Bayesian prior, among all BIC algorithms.
