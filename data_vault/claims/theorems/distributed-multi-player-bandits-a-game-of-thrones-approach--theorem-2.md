# Theorem 2

Define $\pmb{a}^{k*} = \arg \max_{\pmb{a}} \sum_{n=1}^{N} u_n(\pmb{a})$. Under the GoT dynamics, the unique stochastically stable state is $z^* = [a^{k*}, C^N]$ with probability 1.
