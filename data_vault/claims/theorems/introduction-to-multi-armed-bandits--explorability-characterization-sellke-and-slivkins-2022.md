# Explorability Characterization (Sellke and Slivkins 2022)

For independent priors, explorability is completely determined by the arms' priors, and admits a simple characterization: each arm $a$ is explorable if and only if $\Pr\left[\,\mu_{a^{\prime}}<\mu_{a}^{0}\,\right]>0$ for all arms $a^{\prime}\neq a$.
