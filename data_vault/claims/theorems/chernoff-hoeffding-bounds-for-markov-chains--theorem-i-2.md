# Theorem I.2

Under the same setting as Theorem I.1, for any δ ∈ (0, 1], Pr[X ≥ (1+δ)μ] ≤ (‖φ/π‖_π) · exp(−(1−λ(M))μδ²/3) and Pr[X ≤ (1−δ)μ] ≤ (‖φ/π‖_π) · exp(−(1−λ(M))μδ²/2)
