# Refined Competitive Ratio (Kesselheim and Singla 2020)

Kesselheim and Singla (2020) refine the analysis of $\mathtt{LagrangeBwK}$ to achieve competitive ratio $O\left(\,\log(d)\,\log(T)\,\right)$, and prove that such competitive ratio is optimal up to constant factors.
