# Besbes and Zeevi (2012) multi-product regret

Besbes and Zeevi (2012) provide several algorithms with non-adaptive exploration for the regime when all budgets are Ω(T). In particular, they attain regret Õ(T^{1-1/(n+3)}) when demands are Lipschitz (as a function of prices) and expected revenue is concave (as a function of demands).
