# Multi-product dynamic pricing with Lipschitz demands

Dynamic pricing with n ≥ 2 products: if the demand curve is Lipschitz, one can use discretization and run an optimal BwK algorithm on a pre-selected finite subset P of price vectors, bound the discretization error, and achieve regret rate on the order of T^{(n+1)/(n+2)}.
