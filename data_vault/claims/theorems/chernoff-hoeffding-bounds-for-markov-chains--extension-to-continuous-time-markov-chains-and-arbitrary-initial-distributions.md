# Extension to continuous-time Markov chains and arbitrary initial distributions

Both the Chernoff-Hoeffding bounds based on L_1 mixing-time and spectral expansion extend to continuous-time Markov chains, and to the case where the walk starts from an arbitrary distribution x, at a price of a multiplicative factor depending on the distribution x in the concentration bounds.
