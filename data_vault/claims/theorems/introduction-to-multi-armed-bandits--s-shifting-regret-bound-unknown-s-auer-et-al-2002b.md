# S-shifting regret bound (unknown S, Auer et al. 2002b)

Auer et al. (2002b) obtain $\operatornamewithlimits{\mathbb{E}}[R_S(T)] = \tilde{O}(S\sqrt{KT})$ if $S$ is not known.
