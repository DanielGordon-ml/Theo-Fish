# Lower bound (108)

There is a nearly matching lower bound which holds for any given triple of parameters $K,T,|\Pi|$: $\operatornamewithlimits{\mathbb{E}}[R(T)]\geq\min\left(T,\;\Omega\left(\sqrt{KT\log(|\Pi|)/\log(K)}\right)\right).$
