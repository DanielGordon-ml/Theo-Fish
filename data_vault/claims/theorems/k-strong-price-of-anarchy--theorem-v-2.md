# Theorem V.2

Any resource allocation problem (G, W) ∈ 𝒢_n × {w} with the utility rule ũ_ζ is (1, ρ̃_ζ - 1)-k-generalized-coalitionally smooth, where ũ_ζ and ρ̃_ζ are solutions to the linear program (Qζ).
