# Impossibility result for cBwK with small budget

In a stark contrast with BwK, non-trivial regret bounds are not necessarily achievable when B and OPT_Π are small. Indeed, o(OPT) worst-case regret is impossible in the regime OPT_Π ≤ B ≤ √(KT)/2 (Badanidiyuru et al., 2014). Whereas o(OPT) regret bound holds for BwK whenever B = ω(1), as per (148).
