# Theorem IV.1

Consider a common interest game G with system welfare W and n agents. Given any initial joint action a(0) ∈ 𝒜, the k-coalitional round-robin dynamics converge to a k-strong Nash equilibrium in at most ∏_{i=1}^{n} |𝒜_i| iterations. The number of welfare evaluations required per iteration is at most ∑_{C ∈ 𝒞_k} ∏_{i ∈ C} |𝒜_i|.
