# BCO with knapsacks regret bound via LagrangeBwK

LagrangeBwK reduction in Immorlica et al. (2022) yields a regret bound of the form (T/B)√T · poly(K log T) for bandit convex optimization with knapsacks, building on the recent breakthrough in BCO in Bubeck et al. (2017).
