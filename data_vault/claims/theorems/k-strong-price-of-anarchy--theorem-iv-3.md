# Theorem IV.3

Consider a (λ, μ)-k-coalitionally smooth system (G, W), the asynchronous k-coalitional best response dynamics with probability distribution p over group sizes, and let the resulting Markov chain be M. Then, for any initial joint action a(0) and time horizon T, the expected cumulative welfare satisfies E[∑_{t=0}^{T-1} W(a(t))] ≥ (λ/(1+μ)) · T · W(a*) − (1/(min_j p_j)) · W(a*), where a* is the optimal joint action and p_j are the probabilities of selecting groups of various sizes.
