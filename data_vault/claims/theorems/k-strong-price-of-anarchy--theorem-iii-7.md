# Theorem III.7

Consider the class of resource allocation problems 𝒢_n with local welfare rule w and coalition size k. Then, the k-strong price of anarchy bound for the class 𝒢_n with welfare rule w is given by the solution to the combined smoothness optimization problem P[k], which can be computed via a tractable linear program.
