# Theorem IV.2

The Asynchronous k-Coalitional Best-Response Dynamics converge almost surely to the set of k-strong Nash equilibrium. Further, if (G,W) is (λ,μ)-k-coalitionally smooth, then after T update steps, the cumulative expected welfare satisfies 𝔼[1/T ∑_{t=1}^T W(a^t)] ≥ (T-1)/(2T) * (∑_{ζ=1}^k p_ζ λ_ζ) / (1+∑_{ζ=1}^k p_ζ μ_ζ) * W(a^opt).
