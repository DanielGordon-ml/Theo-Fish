# Optimality of logarithmic regret bound (11)

The logarithmic regret bound in (11) is optimal up to constant factors. A line of work strived to optimize the multiplicative constant in O(). Auer et al. (2002a); Bubeck (2010); Garivier and Cappé (2011) analyze this constant for UCB1, and eventually improve it to 1/(2 ln 2). This factor is the best possible in view of the lower bound in Section 12.
