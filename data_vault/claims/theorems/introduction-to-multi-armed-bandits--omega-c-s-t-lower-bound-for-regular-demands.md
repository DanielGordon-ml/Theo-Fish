# Ω(c_S · √T) lower bound for regular demands

Babaioff et al. (2015a) provide a matching Ω(c_S · √T) lower bound, even if an S-dependent constant c_S is allowed in Ω(·).


## Proof

The lower bound is proved in Babaioff et al. (2015a) via a reduction to the respective lower bounds from Kleinberg and Leighton (2003) for the unconstrained case (B=T).
