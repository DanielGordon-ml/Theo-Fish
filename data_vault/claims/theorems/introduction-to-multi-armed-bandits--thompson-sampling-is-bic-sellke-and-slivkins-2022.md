# Thompson Sampling is BIC (Sellke and Slivkins 2022)

Thompson Sampling is BIC for independent priors (and any $K$), when initialized with prior $\mathcal{P}$ and a sufficient warm-start. If prior mean rewards are the same for all arms, then Thompson Sampling is BIC even without the warm-start.
