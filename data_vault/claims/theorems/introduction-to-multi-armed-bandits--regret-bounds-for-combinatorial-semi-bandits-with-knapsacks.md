# Regret bounds for combinatorial semi-bandits with knapsacks

For combinatorial semi-bandits with knapsacks, the regret bound (155) for linear cBwK applies, yielding Õ(m√T)(1 + OPT/B). Sankararaman and Slivkins (2018) achieve an improved regret bound when the set system F is a matroid. In the regime when min(B, OPT) > Ω(T), these two regret bounds become, respectively, Õ(m√T) and Õ(√(mT)). The √(mT) regret is optimal, up to constant factors, even without resources.
