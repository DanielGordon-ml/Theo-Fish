# Regret bounds for pseudo-regret in incentivized exploration (Mansour et al. 2020)

Mansour et al. (2020) achieve regret bounds with a near-optimal dependence on $T$, both in the $\tilde{O}(\sqrt{T})$ worst-case sense and in the $O(\log T)$ instance-dependent sense, for pseudo-regret guarantees for each realization of the prior in incentivized exploration.
