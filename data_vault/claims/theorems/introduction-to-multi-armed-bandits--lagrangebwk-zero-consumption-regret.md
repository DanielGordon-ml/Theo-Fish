# LagrangeBwK Zero-Consumption Regret

On instances with zero resource consumption, the $\mathtt{LagrangeBwK}$ algorithm (with randomized $\gamma$) achieves $\tilde{O}(\sqrt{KT})$ regret, for any choice of parameter $\gamma$.
