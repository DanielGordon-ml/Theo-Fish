# Lemma II.4

For an ergodic Markov chain M and any distribution x on [n], ‖xM/π − 1‖_π ≤ λ(M)‖x/π − 1‖_π. That is, ‖(xM − π)/π‖_π ≤ λ(M)‖(x − π)/π‖_π.
