# Lemma II.5

For an ergodic Markov chain N with state space [n], stationary distribution π and spectral expansion λ(N), the ε-mixing time satisfies T(ε) ≤ ⌈log(1/ε) / log(1/λ(N))⌉ for all ε > 0.
