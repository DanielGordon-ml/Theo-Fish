# Lemma 5

(The effect of the M operator) Let M be an ergodic Markov chain with state space [n], stationary distribution π, and spectral expansion λ = λ(M). Then
1. πM = π.
2. For every vector y with y ⊥ π, we have yM ⊥ π and ‖yM‖_π ≤ λ‖y‖_π.
