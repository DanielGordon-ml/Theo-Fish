# Lemma II.3

For an ergodic Markov chain M, let M̃ denote its time-reversal and R = M̃M denote the multiplicative reversiblization. Then λ(M) = √(λ(R)) = √(1 − γ(R)), where γ(R) denotes the spectral gap of R.
