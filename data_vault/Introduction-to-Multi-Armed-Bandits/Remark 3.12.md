# Remark 3.12

Lemma 3.10 does not rely on any specific structure of the prior. Moreover, it can be used to upper-bound Bayesian regret of Thompson Sampling for a particular *class* of priors whenever one has “nice” confidence bounds $U$ and $L$ for this class.
