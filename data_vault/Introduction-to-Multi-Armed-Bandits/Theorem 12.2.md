# Theorem 12.2

Eq. (174) holds, for appropriate $\beta$, in the following cases: (a) Bounded intervals: $X_{i}\in[a_{i},b_{i}]$ for all $i\in[n]$, and $\beta=\tfrac{1}{n}\sum_{i\in[n]}(b_{i}-a_{i})^{2}$. (b) Bounded variance: $X_{i}\in[0,1]$ and $\mathtt{Variance}(X_{i})\leq\beta/8$ for all $i\in[n]$. (c) Gaussians: Each $X_{i}$, $i\in[n]$ is Gaussian with variance at most $\beta/4$.
