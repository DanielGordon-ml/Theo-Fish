# Remark 5.10

This bound is very meaningful if $\mathtt{cost}^{*}$ is small, but it does not imply sublinear regret guarantees when $\mathtt{cost}^{*}=\Omega(T)$. Interestingly, it recovers the $O(\ln K)$ number of mistakes in the special case with a perfect expert, i.e., when $\mathtt{cost}^{*}=0.$
