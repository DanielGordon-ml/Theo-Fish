# Exercise 10.2

Recall that $\mathtt{OPT_{FA}}$ and $\mathtt{OPT_{FD}}$ are, resp., the fixed-arm benchmark and the fixed-distribution benchmark, as defined in Section\xa055. Let $d$ be the number of resources, including time.
- (a)
Construct an example such that
$\mathtt{OPT_{FD}}\geq d\cdot\mathtt{OPT_{FA}}-o(\mathtt{OPT_{FA}})$.
Hint: Extend the $d=2$ example from Section\xa055.
- (b)
Prove that
$\mathtt{OPT}\leq d\cdot\mathtt{OPT_{FA}}.$
Hint: (141) has an optimal solution with support size at most $d$. Use the pigeonhole principle!
