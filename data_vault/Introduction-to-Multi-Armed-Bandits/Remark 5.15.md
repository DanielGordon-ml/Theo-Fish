# Remark 5.15

We also obtain a meaningful performance guarantee which holds with probability $1$, rather than in expectation. Using the same parameters, $\alpha=\epsilon=\sqrt{\tfrac{\ln K}{T}}$ and $\beta=0$, and assuming all costs are at most $1$, Eq.\xa0(82) implies that $\displaystyle\textstyle\sum_{t\in[T]}p_{t}\cdot c_{t}-\mathtt{cost}^{*}<2\cdot\sqrt{T\ln{K}}.$ (84)
