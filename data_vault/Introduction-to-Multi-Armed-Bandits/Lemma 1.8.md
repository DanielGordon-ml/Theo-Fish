# Lemma 1.8

For two arms, Algorithm 3 achieves regret $\operatornamewithlimits{\mathbb{E}}\left[\,R(t)\,\right]\leq O\left(\,\sqrt{t\log T}\,\right)$ for each round $t\leq T$.
