# Remark 6.9

This analysis extends to $\gamma=0$. In other words, the uniform exploration step in the algorithm is not necessary. While we previously used $\gamma>0$ to guarantee that $q_{t}(a_{t,e})>0$ for each expert $e$, the same conclusion also follows from the fact that $\mathtt{Hedge}$ chooses each expert with a non-zero probability.
