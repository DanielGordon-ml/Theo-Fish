# Fact 11.6

Suppose random variable $Z$ is determined by $Y$ and some other random variable $Z_{0}$ such that $X$ and $Z_{0}$ are independent (think of $Z_{0}$ as algorithm’s random seed). Then $\operatornamewithlimits{\mathbb{E}}[\,\operatornamewithlimits{\mathbb{E}}[X|Y]\,\mid Z]=\operatornamewithlimits{\mathbb{E}}[X|Z]$.
