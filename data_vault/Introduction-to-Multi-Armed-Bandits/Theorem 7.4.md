# Theorem 7.4

Consider the online routing problem with semi-bandit feedback. Assume deterministic oblivious adversary. Algorithm $\mathtt{SemiBanditHedge}$ achieved regret $\operatornamewithlimits{\mathbb{E}}[R(T)]\leq O(d^{3/2}\;T^{3/4})$.
