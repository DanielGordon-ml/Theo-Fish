# Definition 4.4

A subset $S \subset X$ is called an $\epsilon$-mesh, $\epsilon>0$, if every point $x \in X$ is within distance $\epsilon$ from $S$, in the sense that $\mathcal{D}(x,y) \leq \epsilon$ for some $y \in S$.
