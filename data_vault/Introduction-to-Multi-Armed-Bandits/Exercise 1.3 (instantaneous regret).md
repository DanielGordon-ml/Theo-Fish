# Exercise 1.3 (instantaneous regret)

Recall that instantaneous regret at round $t$ is $\Delta(a_{t})=\mu^{*}-\mu(a_{t})$.
- (a) Prove that Successive Elimination achieves “instance-independent” regret bound of the form $\displaystyle\operatornamewithlimits{\mathbb{E}}\left[\,\Delta(a_{t})\,\right]\leq\frac{\operatornamewithlimits{polylog}(T)}{\sqrt{t/K}}\quad\text{for each round $t\in[T]$}.$ (16)
- (b) Derive an “instance-independent” upper bound on instantaneous regret of Explore-first.
