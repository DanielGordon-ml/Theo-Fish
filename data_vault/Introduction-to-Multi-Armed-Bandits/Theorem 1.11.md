# Theorem 1.11

Successive Elimination algorithm achieves regret $\displaystyle\operatornamewithlimits{\mathbb{E}}[R(T)]\leq O(\log{T})\left[\,\sum_{\text{arms $a$ with $\mu(a)<\mu(a^{*})$}}\frac{1}{\mu(a^{*})-\mu(a)}\,\right].$
