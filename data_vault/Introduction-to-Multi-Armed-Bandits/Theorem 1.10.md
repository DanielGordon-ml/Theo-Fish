# Theorem 1.10

Successive Elimination algorithm achieves regret $\displaystyle\operatornamewithlimits{\mathbb{E}}\left[\,R(t)\,\right]=O\left(\,\sqrt{Kt\log T}\,\right)\quad\text{for all rounds $t\leq T$}.$
