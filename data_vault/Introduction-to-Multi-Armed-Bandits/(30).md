# (30)

$\displaystyle|P_{0}^{*}(A)-P_{j}^{*}(A)|\leq\epsilon\sqrt{m}<\tfrac{1}{8}\quad\text{for all events $A\subset\Omega^{*}$}.$

## Proof

By applying Pinsker's inequality and the Chain Rule for KL-divergence, and using Theorem~\ref{LB:thm:KL-props}(d), we have $\mathtt{KL}(P_{0}^{*},P_{j}^{*}) \leq m \cdot 2\epsilon^{2}$, leading to the inequality under the assumption $T\leq\frac{cK}{\epsilon^{2}}$ with small enough constant $c$.
