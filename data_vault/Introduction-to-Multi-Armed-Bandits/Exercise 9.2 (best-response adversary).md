# Exercise 9.2 (best-response adversary)

The best-response adversary, as defined in Eq.\xa0(122), can be seen as an algorithm in the setting of Chapter\xa051. (More formally, this algorithm knows $\mathtt{ALG}$ and the game matrix $M$, and receives auxiliary feedback $\mathcal{F}_{t}=j_{t}$.)
Prove that its expected regret satisfies $\operatornamewithlimits{\mathbb{E}}[R^{\prime}(T)]\leq 0$.

Take-away: Thus, Theorem\xa09.5 applies to the best-response adversary, with $\operatornamewithlimits{\mathbb{E}}[R^{\prime}(T)]\leq 0$.
