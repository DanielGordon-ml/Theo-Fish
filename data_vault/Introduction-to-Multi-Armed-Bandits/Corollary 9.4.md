# Corollary 9.4

$M(p^{*},q^{*})=v^{*}$, and the pair $(p^{*},q^{*})$ forms a *Nash equilibrium*, in the sense that $p^{*}\in\operatornamewithlimits{argmin}_{p\in\Delta_{\mathtt{rows}}}M(p,q^{*})$ and $q^{*}\in\operatornamewithlimits{argmax}_{q\in\Delta_{\mathtt{cols}}}M(p^{*},q)$ .
