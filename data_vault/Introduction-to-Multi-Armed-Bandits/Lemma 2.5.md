# Lemma 2.5

Consider sample space $\Omega=\{0,1\}^{n}$ and two distributions on $\Omega$, $p=\mathtt{RC}_{\epsilon}^{n}$ and $q=\mathtt{RC}_{0}^{n}$, for some $\epsilon>0$.
Then
$|p(A)-q(A)|\leq\epsilon\,\sqrt{n}$
for any event $A\subset\Omega$.
