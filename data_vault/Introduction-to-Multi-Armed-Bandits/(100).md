# (100)

A natural benchmark is the best-response policy, $\pi^{*}(x)=\max_{a\in\mathcal{A}}\mu(a|x)$. Then regret is defined as $$ R(T)=\mathtt{REW}(\pi^{*})-\mathtt{REW}(\mathtt{ALG}). $$
