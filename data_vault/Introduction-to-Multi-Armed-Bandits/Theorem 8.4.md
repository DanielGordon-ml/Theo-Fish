# Theorem 8.4

Consider the Lipschitz contextual bandits problem with contexts in $[0,1]$. The uniform discretization algorithm (102) yields regret $\operatornamewithlimits{\mathbb{E}}[R(T)]=O(T^{2/3}(LK\ln T)^{1/3})$.
