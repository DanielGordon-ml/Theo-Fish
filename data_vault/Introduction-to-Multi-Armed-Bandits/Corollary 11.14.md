# Corollary 11.14

$\mathtt{RepeatedHE}$ is BIC if $\epsilon<\tfrac{1}{3}\,\operatornamewithlimits{\mathbb{E}}\left[G_{t}\cdot{\bf 1}_{\left\{\,G_{t}>0\,\right\}}\right]$ for each time $t>N_{0}$.
