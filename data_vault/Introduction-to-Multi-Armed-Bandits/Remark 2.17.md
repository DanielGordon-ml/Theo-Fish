# Remark 2.17

The lower bound from part (a) is similar to the upper bound achieved by UCB1 and Successive Elimination: $R(T)\leq\sum_{a:\;\Delta(a)>0}\;\frac{O(\log T)}{\Delta(a)}$. In particular, we see that the upper bound is optimal up to a constant factor when $\mu^{*}$ is bounded away from 0 and $1$, e.g., when $\mu^{*}\in\left[\,\icefrac{{1}}{{4}},\icefrac{{3}}{{4}}\,\right]$.
