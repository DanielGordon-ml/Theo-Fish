# Claim 7.15

Fix $v_{t}\in[0,U/d]^{d}$. There exists a random variable $v^{\prime}_{0}\in\mathbb{R}^{d}$ such that (i) $v^{\prime}_{0}$ and $v_{0}+v_{t}$ have the same marginal distribution, and (ii) $\Pr[v^{\prime}_{0}\neq v_{0}]\leq\epsilon U.$
