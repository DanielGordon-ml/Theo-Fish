# Theorem 7.7

Consider combinatorial semi-bandits with deterministic oblivious adversary. Then algorithm $\mathtt{SemiBanditFTPL}$ with appropriately chosen parameter $\gamma$ achieved regret
$$ \operatornamewithlimits{\mathbb{E}}[R(T)]\leq O\left(d^{5/4}\;T^{3/4}\right). $$
