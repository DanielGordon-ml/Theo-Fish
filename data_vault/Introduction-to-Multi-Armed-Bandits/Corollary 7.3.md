# Corollary 7.3

Consider online routing with full feedback. Algorithm $\mathtt{Hedge}$ with with parameter $\epsilon=1/\sqrt{dT}$ achieves regret $\operatornamewithlimits{\mathbb{E}}[R(T)]\leq O(d\sqrt{dT})$.
