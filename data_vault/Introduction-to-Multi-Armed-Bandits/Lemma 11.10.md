Algorithm 1 is BIC, for any target function $a_{\mathtt{trg}}$, as long as $\epsilon\leq\tfrac{1}{3}\,\operatornamewithlimits{\mathbb{E}}\left[G\cdot{\bf 1}_{\left\{\,G>0\,\right\}}\right]$.
