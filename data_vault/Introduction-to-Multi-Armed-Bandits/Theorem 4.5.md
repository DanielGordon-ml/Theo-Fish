# Theorem 4.5

Consider Lipschitz bandits with time horizon $T$. Optimizing over the choice of an $\epsilon$-mesh, uniform discretization with algorithm $\mathtt{ALG}$ satisfying (52) attains regret $\displaystyle \operatornamewithlimits{\mathbb{E}}[R(T)]\leq\inf_{\epsilon>0,\;\text{$\epsilon$-mesh $S$}}\;\epsilon T+c_{\mathtt{ALG}}\cdot\sqrt{|S|\,T\log T}.$ (58)
