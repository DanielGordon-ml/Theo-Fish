# (25)

There are $\geq\tfrac{2K}{3}$ arms $j$ such that $\displaystyle{\textstyle\operatornamewithlimits{\mathbb{E}}_{0}}(T_{j})\leq\tfrac{3T}{K},$

## Proof

To prove (25), assume for contradiction that we have more than $\frac{K}{3}$ arms with $\operatornamewithlimits{\mathbb{E}}_{0}(T_{j})>\frac{3T}{K}$. Then the expected total number of times these arms are played is strictly greater than $T$, which is a contradiction.
