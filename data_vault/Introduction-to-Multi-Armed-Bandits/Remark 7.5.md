# Remark 7.5

Fake cost $\widehat{c}_{t}(e)$ is determined by the corresponding true cost $c_{t}(e)$ and event $\Lambda_{t,e}$ which does not depend on algorithm’s actions. Therefore, fake costs are chosen by a (randomized) oblivious adversary. In particular, in order to apply Theorem 7.1 with a different algorithm $\mathtt{ALG}$ for online learning with experts, it suffices to have an upper bound on regret against an oblivious adversary.
