# Exercise 4.8 (uniform discretization)

Use (72) to recover the regret bound for uniform discretization: with any algorithm $\mathtt{ALG}$ satisfying (52) and discretization step $\epsilon=(T/\log T)^{-1/3}$ one obtains $$ \operatornamewithlimits{\mathbb{E}}[R(T)]\leq T^{2/3}\cdot(1+c_{\mathtt{ALG}})(\log T)^{1/3}. $$
