# Exercise 10.5

Consider Adversarial $\mathtt{BwK}$. Prove that $\mathtt{OPT_{FA}}$ can be arbitrarily worse than $\mathtt{OPT_{FD}}$. Specifically, fix arbitrary time horizon $T$, budget $B<T/2$, and number of arms $K$, and construct a problem instance with $\mathtt{OPT_{FA}}=0$ and $\mathtt{OPT_{FD}}>\Omega(T)$.

Hint: Make all arms have reward 0 and consumption $1$ in the first $B$ rounds.
