# Remark 5.5

Pseudo-regret cannot exceed regret, because the best-in-foresight arm is a weaker benchmark. Some positive results for pseudo-regret carry over to regret, and some don’t. For IID rewards/costs, the $\sqrt{T}$ upper regret bounds from Chapter 1 extend to regret, whereas the $\log(T)$ upper regret bounds do not extend in full generality, see Exercise 5.2 for details.
