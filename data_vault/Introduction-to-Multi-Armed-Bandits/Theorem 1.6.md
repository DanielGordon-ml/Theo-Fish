# Theorem 1.6

Epsilon-greedy algorithm with exploration probabilities $\epsilon_{t}=t^{-1/3}\cdot(K\log t)^{1/3}$ achieves regret bound $\operatornamewithlimits{\mathbb{E}}[R(t)]\leq t^{2/3}\cdot O(K\log t)^{1/3}$ for each round $t$.
