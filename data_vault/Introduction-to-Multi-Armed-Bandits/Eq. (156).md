# Eq. (156)

$$G_{\mathtt{LAG}}(a):=\mathtt{OPT}_{\mathtt{LP}}-\mathcal{L}(a,\lambda^{*})\qquad\text{\emph{(Lagrangian gap of arm $a$)}},$ where $\lambda^{*}$ is a minimizer dual vector in Eq.\xa0(144), and $G_{\mathtt{LAG}}:=\textstyle\min_{a\not\in\{a^{*},\mathtt{null}\}}G_{\mathtt{LAG}}(a)$.
