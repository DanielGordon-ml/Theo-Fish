# (27)

There are at least $\frac{K}{3}$ arms $j$ such that $\Pr\left[\,T_{j}\leq\tfrac{24T}{K}\,\right]\geq\frac{7}{8}\text{ and }P_{0}(y_{T}=j)\leq\frac{3}{K}.$
