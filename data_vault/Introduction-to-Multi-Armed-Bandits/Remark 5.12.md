# Remark 5.12

Note that the special case of *binary* prediction with experts advice is much easier for deterministic algorithms. Indeed, it allows for an “approximation ratio” arbitrarily close to $2$, as in Theorem 5.9, whereas in the general case the “approximation ratio” cannot be better than $K$.
