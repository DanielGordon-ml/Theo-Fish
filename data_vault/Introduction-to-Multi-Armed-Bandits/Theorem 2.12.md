# Theorem 2.12

In the setup of Theorem 2.11, suppose $\operatornamewithlimits{\mathbb{E}}[R(T)]\leq C\cdot T^{\gamma}$ for all problem instances, for some numbers $\gamma\in[\icefrac{{2}}{{3}},1)$ and $C>0$. Then for any problem instance a random permutation of arms yields $\operatornamewithlimits{\mathbb{E}}\left[\,R(T)\,\right]\textstyle\geq\Omega\left(\,C^{-2}\cdot T^{\lambda}\cdot\sum_{a}\Delta(a)\,\right),\quad\text{where $\lambda=2(1-\gamma)$.}$
