# Exercise 1.1 (rewards from a small interval)

Consider a version of the problem in which all the realized rewards are in the interval $\left[\,\icefrac{{1}}{{2}},\icefrac{{1}}{{2}}+\epsilon\,\right]$ for some $\epsilon\in(0,\icefrac{{1}}{{2}})$. Define versions of $\mathtt{UCB1}$ and Successive Elimination attain improved regret bounds (both logarithmic and root-T) that depend on the $\epsilon$.
