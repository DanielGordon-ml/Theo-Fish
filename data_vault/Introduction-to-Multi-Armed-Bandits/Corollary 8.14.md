# Corollary 8.14

Consider the setting in Lemma 8.13. Fix policy class $\Pi$ and let $\pi_{0}\in\operatornamewithlimits{argmax}_{\pi\in\Pi}\mathtt{IPS}(\pi)$. Then for each $\delta>0$, with probability at least $\delta>0$ we have $\displaystyle\max_{\pi\in\Pi}\tilde{r}(\pi)-\tilde{r}(\pi_{0})\leq O\left(\sqrt{\tfrac{1}{p_{0}}\;\log(\tfrac{|\Pi|}{\delta})/N}\right).$
