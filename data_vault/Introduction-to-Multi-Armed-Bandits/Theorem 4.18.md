# Theorem 4.18

Consider Lipschitz bandits with time horizon $T$. Assume that realized rewards take values on a finite set. For any given problem instance and any $c>0$, the zooming algorithm attains regret $\displaystyle\operatornamewithlimits{\mathbb{E}}[R(T)]\leq O\left(\,T^{(d+1)/(d+2)}\;(c\log T)^{1/(d+2)}\,\right),$ where $d$ is the zooming dimension with multiplier $c$.
