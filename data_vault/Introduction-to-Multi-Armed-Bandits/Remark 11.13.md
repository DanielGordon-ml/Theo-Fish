# Remark 11.13

$\mathtt{RepeatedHE}$ can be seen as a reduction from bandit algorithms to BIC bandit algorithms. The simplest version always chooses arm $2$ in exploration rounds, and (only) provides non-adaptive exploration. For better regret bounds, $\mathtt{ALG}$ needs to perform adaptive exploration, as per Chapter 1.
