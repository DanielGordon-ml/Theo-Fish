# Definition 4.17

For an instance of Lipschitz MAB, the *zooming dimension* with multiplier $c>0$ is $\inf_{d\geq 0}\left\{N_{r/3}(X)\leq c\cdot r^{-d}\quad\forall r>0\right\}$.
