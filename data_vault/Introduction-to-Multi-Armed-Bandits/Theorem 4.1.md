# Theorem 4.1

Consider continuum-armed bandits with Lipschitz constant $L$ and time horizon $T$. Uniform discretization with algorithm $\mathtt{ALG}$ satisfying (52) and discretization step $\epsilon=(TL^{2}/\log T)^{-1/3}$ attains $$ \operatornamewithlimits{\mathbb{E}}[R(T)]\leq L^{1/3}\cdot T^{2/3}\cdot(1+c_{\mathtt{ALG}})(\log T)^{1/3}. $$
